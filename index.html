About Image Feed Pro

Image Feed Pro integrates the most recently top tagged pictures on Instagram in order for you to view them on your device. There are several links on the main menu of the top 20 tags of 2014 that are viewable with a single click, or you can use the handy search box to see photos of any tag that you'd like!

Below you have two options of how you can use Image Feed Pro.

  • If you do have an account with Instagram click the corresponding button (This will verify your membership and allow you to do what any Instagram account user can do)
  • If You don't have an account with Instagram, then click the corresponding button. (You will still be able to search and view the awesomeness of Instagram, just without the extra privileges)
